SOFIA

PhotoLeón


SOFIA is an acronym in Spanish that stands for System of Photographic Organization and Administrative Integration.

Dashboard for company-wide data management

Sofia's dashboard allows employees to access data and configure important parameters for the company. Each work area has access to specific tools in its dashboard to facilitate the work.

WhatsApp Business to maintain direct communication with customers

For PhotoLeón it is important to maintain good communication with customers, WhatApp Business allows us to keep them updated on their payments and delivery processes as well as allows us to solve their doubts quickly.

Face Recognition System to filter millions of photos every month
